Description

Enmoladas are a traditional Mexican dish. Rich and complex in flavor, enmoladas are best known for the mole sauce, which is a blend of various chili peppers, spices, and chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of chicken, roasted and shredded
  • 1/2 cup of Mexican Crema
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of thinly sliced onion
  • 4 cups of chicken stock
  • 4 tsp. of brown sugar
  • 1 cup of mole sauce
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1/4 cup of toasted pepitas
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled queso fresco
  • 1/4 cup of thinly sliced onion

Steps

  1. Pour the chicken stock into a wide pan and bring to a boil.
  2. Add the mole sauce and chocolate triangles. Stir. When the sauce and chocolate are fully dissolved in the stock, turn heat to medium low.
  3. One by one, submerge the tortillas into the piping-hot mole rojo. Place each tortilla on a plate, open, and spoon 2 to 3 Tbsps shredded chicken into its center.
  4. Over the chicken, sprinkle 1 to 2 Tbsps shredded chicken into its center.
  5. Over the chicken, sprinkle 1 to 2 Tbps of mozzarella cheese, 1 tsp. of cream, and 1 to 2 Tbsps of the sliced onions.
  6. Fold each tortilla into a thin roll (like a taquito) and garnish with a sprinkling of queso fresco, onion slivers, crema, and toasted pepitas.
